How are you feeling right now?

Answering a few simple questions can help you understand more about yourself. Take a quick mental health screening to get a snapshot of your current state of mind, as well as some recommendations for the next steps.

The screening is a combination of the Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7 (GAD-7) and Patient Health Questionnaire 8 (PHQ-8). This is not a substitute for care by a licensed healthcare provider. Stress is a common part of life, but it can become overwhelming. Managing stress can improve your health and well-being. Try meditation, exercise, and talking to someone to reduce stress . If you're feeling overwhelmed, don't hesitate to seek help from a specialist.
